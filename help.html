Help

Use arrowes to move with your shark.
Your task is to catch as much as possible fish with right answer on question in the bottom of the page.
Wrong fish decrease your score about 1 point and Right fish increase your score.

There are also some bonus:
Seal increase your time about 3 seconds.
Octopus decrease your time about 3 seconds.
Bombs end your game.
Right arrow + space = speed up
